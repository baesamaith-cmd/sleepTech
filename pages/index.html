Quick reset

When sleep feels stuck

  • Use bed mainly for sleep, not for scrolling, planning, or worrying.
  • If you are not drifting off after about 20 minutes, get out of bed for a brief quiet reset.
  • Return only when you feel sleepy again.
  • Keep your wake time as consistent as you reasonably can.

Use caution

Sleep restriction is not casual advice

Compression or restriction approaches can help some people, but they can also raise daytime sleepiness. Be careful if you have driving risk, seizures, bipolar disorder, severe depression or anxiety, or possible sleep apnea.
